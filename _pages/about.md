This website is my Capstone Portfolio submission to partially fulfill the requirements for the Master of Arts in Library and Information Science (MLIS) degree from the School of Library and Information Science (SLIS) at the University of Iowa (UI). The content of the various webpages highlights samples of the work that I have done and aligns them with the SLIS Core Competences, which are based on ALA's Core Competences of Librarianship policy.

To navigate this website, use the headers at the top fo the page and in the menu bar on the rop right. My professional statement and resume can be found under "About Me". Please click through the links on the "Work Samples" page to find essays and projects with accompanying professional development goals. The "Just for Fun" page is exactly that and contains links to content I discovered during my educational exploits that I enjoyed or that resonated with me.

Many thanks to Dr. Matthew Schramm for his patience and technical knowledge while helping me with the layout of this website. Any errors are my own.

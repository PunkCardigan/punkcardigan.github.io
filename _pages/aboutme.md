The only thing I like more than knowing things and solving problems is being able to help other people do the same. With an anthropological perspective of the world and nearly two decades of customer service experience, I excel at communicating with people from all walks of life and have found my niche in libraries. I am a naturally friendly person who genuinely enjoys making the public spaces of a library as welcoming as my own home. There should be something for everyone at a library and I am dedicated to promoting diverse and accessible library collections and developing a catalog with useful records that meet the needs of all members of the community.
